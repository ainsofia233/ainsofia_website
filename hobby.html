My Hobbies

Reading

Reading isn’t just a hobby, it’s my therapy. When life feels overwhelming, I lose myself in fictional worlds, allowing stories to lift my spirits and renew my energy.

Cooking

I view cooking as a way to express care, using my culinary skills to delight and bring joy to others.

Jogging

Embracing a healthy lifestyle has made me more energetic and positive. I make it a habit to jog twice weekly to keep my body and mind in balance.

Food Hunting

I love visiting trending cafés and trying new dishes each month, treating these little food adventures as a reward after a busy month and a moment of pure joy.

Cycling

Cycling with friends at Taman Titiwangsa is my favorite pastime, keeping me active, happy, and fit.

Shopping

Shopping feels like finding treasures everywhere, new items, fresh discoveries, and the scent of the mall make every trip a little adventure.
